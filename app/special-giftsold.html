Special Gifts

SURPRISE YOUR FAMILY AND FRIENDS BY SENDING THEM A GROCERY STORE GIFT CERTIFICATE

• With a grocery store gift certificate your family and friends will be able to shop at The Royal Market for their favorite candies, beverages and other treats during their Cancun or Riviera Maya vacation.
• Grocery store gift certificates are valid at all The Royal Market locations, except The Royal Market at Captains Cove. They are valid for one transaction only and cannot be redeemed for cash. Refunds will not be given if you do not use the full value of the certificate. Certificates are valid only during your stay at Royal Resorts.

ORDER FLOWERS, FRUIT BASKETS, WINE AND CHOCOLATES

A gift of flowers is the perfect way to say “I love you” to someone special and celebrate a birthday, anniversary, Mother’s Day and any other important occasion, even on vacation. Browse the online flower catalog, take your pick and order online. The Royal Market will deliver to the recipient’s suite. Indulge your family and friends even more with surprise gifts for their Cancun vacation. Order a fruit basket, chocolates and candies or a bottle of wine or champagne to toast a special occasion. Orders will be delivered to the Royal Resorts in Cancun and The Royal Haciendas in the Riviera Maya.
